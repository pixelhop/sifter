# Branding Ideas for AI Podcast Digest

## Name Concepts

### Option 1: Focus on "Curation"
- **Sift** - Filtering the good stuff
- **Curate** - Hand-picked selection
- **Picked** - Chosen for you
- **Cull** - Cutting through noise

### Option 2: Focus on "Time/Saving"
- **Digest.fm** - Like a news digest
- **Briefly** - Quick summaries
- **Snippet** - Small pieces
- **Condense** - Compressed knowledge

### Option 3: Focus on "Audio/Sound"
- **Echo** - Repeated/summarized
- **Resonance** - What sticks with you
- **Audibly** - Audio-first
- **Sonic** - Sound focused

### Option 4: Focus on "AI/Personal"
- **PodPal** - Your podcast buddy
- **ListenBot** - AI listener
- **Earworm** - Sticks in your head
- **MyCast** - Personal podcast

### Option 5: Abstract/Unique
- **Fathom** - Deep understanding
- **Tilde** - ~ meaning "approximately/summary"
- **Omnia** - All things
- **Riff** - Musical/pattern reference

## My Top 3 Recommendations

### 1. **Sift** (sift.fm)
- Short, memorable, .fm available
- Meaning: filtering gold from sand
- Tagline: "Sift through the noise"
- Vibe: Clean, modern, editorial

### 2. **Digest** (digest.fm)
- Immediately explains what it does
- Newsletter/digest format is familiar
- Tagline: "Your daily audio digest"
- Vibe: Professional, newsroom, trustworthy

### 3. **Briefly** (briefly.fm)
- Promise of speed/efficiency
- Friendly, conversational
- Tagline: "Briefly, the podcast you need"
- Vibe: Casual, personal assistant

## Visual Direction Ideas

### For "Sift"
- Logo: Abstract sieve/funnel or sound wave filtering
- Colors: Warm gold, charcoal, cream
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Satoshi)
- Vibe: Premium, editorial, calm

### For "Digest"
- Logo: Newspaper/audio wave hybrid
- Colors: Navy, white, accent orange
- Font: Serif headline + sans body (editorial)
- Vibe: Newsroom, trustworthy, morning routine

### For "Briefly"
- Logo: Play button + clock or fast-forward symbol
- Colors: Soft purple, mint, white
- Font: Rounded, friendly (Geist, Manrope)
- Vibe: Friendly, AI assistant, accessible
